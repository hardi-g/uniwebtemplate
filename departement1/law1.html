Department of
Law

Overview and Purpose

  • Overview: Focus on legal studies, principles and regulations.

  • Purpose: Provide legal education, training and promote justice and rule of law.

Department Head

  • Oversees department operations and sets strategic direction.

  • Professors, legal experts, researchers and administrative staff contribute to legal education and research.

Achievements

  • Prominent contributions to legal research and jurisprudence.

  • Notable alumni achievements in legal practice and public service.

  • Successful advocacy for legal reforms or social justice causes.

  • Collaboration with legal institutions and organizations to promote legal excellence.
